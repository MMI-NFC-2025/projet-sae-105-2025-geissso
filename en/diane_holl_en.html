Diane Holl

Diane Holl is an engineer specialized in motorsport. She contributed to the design and evolution of major technical solutions in a field still largely dominated by men.

Carbon fiber chassis & bodywork

Early in her career, Holl completed an internship at Reynard Motorsports where she worked on the first reinforced carbon fiber chassis for Formula 3.

Her accomplishments

Diane Holl contributed to high-level motorsport projects. Her career illustrates the challenges faced by women in gaining recognition in technical roles.
