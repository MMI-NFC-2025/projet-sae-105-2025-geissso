About

Solène, founder of the website

My name is Solène, a student passionate about design, communication and motorsport. This website was created to talk about a topic that is still too little known: the place of women in motorsport.

I have always been fascinated by the determination and talent of those who dare to evolve in male-dominated environments. But over time, I realized that recognition does not always follow their achievements.

Through this website, I want to shed light on these journeys, challenge stereotypes and give visibility back to those whose work is too often erased.

A question of parity and perspective

Talking about women in motorsport is not about opposing genders. It is about restoring balance.

Parity does not mean identical performance, but equal opportunities, resources and recognition.

A message you would like to share?

A testimony, an opinion, a suggestion? This website is meant to be collaborative and evolving.
