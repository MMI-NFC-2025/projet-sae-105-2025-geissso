F1 ACADEMY

★ Matilda Effect / Women in motorsport / The media

Created to give young female drivers a real place in motorsport, F1 Academy is more than a championship: it's a quiet revolution.

What is F1 Academy?

Launched in 2023 by Suzie Wolff, the FIA and Formula One Management, F1 Academy is a 100% female single-seater series of F4 type. It welcomes drivers aged 16 to 25 from around the world, offering them a professional, supported, funded, and above all visible championship.

Each season, five teams (Prema, ART, MP Motorsport, Campos, Rodin) field three cars each, all identical:

  • Engine: Autotune T421
  • Lightweight and secure chassis
  • Pirelli tires
  • Performance close to international F4

The goal is not raw speed, but comprehensive training: driving, communication, pressure management, and media experience.

Top 3 championship 2024

For decades, women have been excluded from professional circuits for lack of talent, but due to lack of adapted structures and financial support.

F1 Academy breaks this cycle:

  • It guarantees accessible budgets
  • Provides access to top-level engineers
  • Offers unprecedented media exposure
  • Creates direct partnership with Formula 1

In 2024, the series gets closer to F1: each Formula 1 team now supports its own F1 Academy driver, with single-seaters in the official team colors.

Result: women are now an integral part of the paddock.
